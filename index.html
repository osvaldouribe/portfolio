Osvaldo Uribe

Experienced product designer with over 15 years of versatile expertise across diverse markets.

My career journey began in Los Angeles at Plural, right after completing my Bachelor's in Graphic Design, while I was still based in Santiago de Chile. This early experience taught me the ropes of remote work and instilled a strong work ethic.

Subsequently, I joined Jelly digital, an agency specializing in Public Relations, which greatly enhanced my communication skills. My role involved creating a wealth of content, especially for Google Plus, a platform from days gone by.

The next chapter in my career led me to Ilogica, where I was entrusted with building their design team and internal processes. This role was an absolute joy; we crafted numerous design systems and websites for a variety of companies.
